About

This website is home to a new fantasy world. One can read chapters as the author writes them. It is fan fiction. The story is fan fiction from Jim Butcher’s book series.

There are photos on the gallery page so that you guys can see what she sees when she talks about the creatures in this fantasy world. Putting a picture to the name of a creature can help you follow the story better as it gets posted.

Everyone is welcome here. Rather you work and have a family or are a single person that enjoys fantasy worlds. Everyone deserves to read what they enjoy.

Share these stories with other sites or social media pages if you enjoy them. Share them with other fantasy lovers and readers too. Check back in for a new post and new chapters to the ongoing story.
