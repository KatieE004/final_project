Author Bio

Katie Eskew is from Illinois and is a student at Florida International University. She is majoring in English: Writing and Rhetoric track Bachelor’s degree. She has been a certified nursing assistant for six years and a nail technician for four years. When she is not working, she is traveling with her family and reading books when she can. She likes reading mysteries, fantasy, literature, horror, and more. She has a love for films and enjoys watching films in her down time. She enjoys writing fan fiction is her free time as well when she is alone.
